Site Purpose

This website will provide information about popular Lagos foods, restaurant recommendations, food events, and contact info for more suggestions.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find the best suya and jollof rice in Lagos?
  • What are the most popular street foods to try as a first-time visitor?
